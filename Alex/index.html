Why Systemutvecklare C/C++ at Chas Academy?

I’ve always been passionate about technology. From building PCs as a kid to working with APIs and integrations as a Configuration Engineer at Unifaun, problem-solving and development have always come naturally to me. My career then took me into product management within e-commerce and logistics, where I led the development of complex systems and learned the value of bridging business and tech.

Alongside my career in tech, music has been a lifelong passion. My interest in audio technology and companies like Neural DSP inspired me to explore how hardware and software integrated products are created, but I realized I needed stronger coding skills to bring my own ideas to life.

That’s why I chose Systemutvecklare C/C++ at Chas Academy. The focus on C, combined with a hands-on, collaborative way of learning, fits me perfectly as a practical learner. This education not only strengthens my technical foundation but also gives me the tools to prototype and develop my own ideas. At the very least, I believe this education will give me a stronger technical foundation for my career, and at best, it will allow me to bring my own ideas to life and collaborate with others who share the same passion for building great things.
